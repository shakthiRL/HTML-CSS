Discover the sweetness of cocoa

Chocolate is one of the most loved treats in the world. It is made from cocoa
beans and comes in many varieties such as milk, dark, and white chocolate.
It can be sweet, bitter, creamy, crunchy, and used in desserts, drinks, and snacks.

How Chocolate is Made

  1. Harvesting Cocoa Beans
  2. Fermenting and Drying
  3. Roasting the Beans
  4. Grinding into Cocoa Mass
  5. Mixing with Milk/Sugar
  6. Molding into Bars
